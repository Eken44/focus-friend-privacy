FOCUS FRIEND - PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: July 4, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Shambles Lab ("we," "our," or "us") handles information in the Focus Friend mobile application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Focus Friend stores data locally on your device only:

  • Tasks you create
  • Timer settings
  • Theme preferences
  • Daily streak count
  • Completion statistics

DATA STORAGE

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

NO DATA SHARING

We do not share, sell, or transmit any user data to third parties.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: shambleslabs@gmail.com
